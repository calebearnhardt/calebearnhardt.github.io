Project Overview

Project Name: Mocksville Barbershop

Project Overview:

This website is for my father Todd who is the owner and head barber at Mocksville Barbershop. This site will let others see the services provided by the barbershop as well as how to contact and much more.

The website will have a page that links to the about page with his bio and experience, a services page about the different services that are offered, a gallery page with some images of his work, and a contact page that gives the information on how to schedule a haircut and see availability.

The intended audience is potential customers.
